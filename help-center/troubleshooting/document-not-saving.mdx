---
title: Document Not Saving
description: Fix issues saving documents in kreaph.
---

## Symptoms

- Edits to docs don't save
- You see a network error

## Possible Causes

- Lost connection to kreaph servers
- Browser extensions interfering

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet">
    ````bash
    ping app.kreaph.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh page">
    Reload kreaph. Resave your edits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable extensions">
    Try saving in private/incognito mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>
