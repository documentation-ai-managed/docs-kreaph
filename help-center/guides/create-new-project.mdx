## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://app.kreaph.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click the "New Project" button in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Fill in project name and description. Click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin Editing">
    Add your documents and start organizing inside your new project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
