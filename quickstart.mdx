## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Internet connection

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card or downloads required. Get started instantly.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for Kreaph in seconds. Choose your preferred method.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    Visit [https://app.kreaph.com/signup](https://app.kreaph.com/signup) and enter your email and password.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user-plus">
        Provide your email, create a strong password, and agree to terms.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify Email">
        Check your inbox for the verification link and click it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log In">
        Return to [https://app.kreaph.com/login](https://app.kreaph.com/login) and sign in.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">
    Click "Continue with Google" on the signup page and authorize access.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      This skips email verification.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, land on the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click the <kbd>New Space</kbd> button in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space">
    Enter a name like "My Project Docs", select "Documentation" template, and choose brand color `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Hit <kbd>Create Space</kbd>. Your space is live at a URL like `https://yourspace.kreaph.com`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Page and Content

Navigate to your new space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Page" icon="file-plus">
    Click <kbd>New Page</kbd> and name it "Introduction".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Content">
    Use the visual editor to add headings, text, and components. Try inserting a code block.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish">
    Click <kbd>Preview</kbd> to see changes, then <kbd>Publish</kbd>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here's an example code block you can add to your page:

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
  ```javascript
  // kreaph client example
  const kreaph = new KreaphClient('YOUR_API_KEY');
  const space = await kreaph.spaces.create({ name: 'My Docs' });
  console.log(`Created space: ${space.url}`);
  ```

  ```python
  # kreaph python example
  from kreaph import KreaphClient
  client = KreaphClient('YOUR_API_KEY')
  space = client.spaces.create(name='My Docs')
  print(f"Created space: {space.url}")
  ```
</CodeGroup>
